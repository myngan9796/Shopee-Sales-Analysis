# 📊 Shopee Sales Performance Analysis (2025)

## Project Overview

This project analyzes the 2025 sales performance of a beauty brand on Shopee using SQL and Power BI. The analysis covers the complete customer journey from store visits to placed orders and confirmed orders, with the goal of identifying business trends, evaluating campaign effectiveness, and providing actionable recommendations.

---

## Business Problem

The company experienced fluctuations in sales performance throughout 2025, especially during major promotional campaigns such as 3.3, 6.6, 9.9, 11.11, and 12.12.

The objective of this project is to answer the following questions:

- How did sales perform throughout 2025?
- Which campaigns generated the highest revenue?
- Did conversion rate improve over time?
- How did customer purchasing behavior change?
- What actions could improve business performance in 2026?

## Key Findings

- Annual Confirmed GMV reached **715.4 Billion VND**
- Overall Conversion Rate was **0.86%**
- 12 campaign days generated **29.6%** of annual GMV
- H2 GMV increased **28.9%** compared with H1
- October was the highest revenue month
- Conversion Rate improved steadily throughout the year
- Basket size declined during mega campaigns despite higher buyer volume

---

## Business Recommendations

- Allocate more resources to mega campaigns (10.10, 11.11, 12.12)
- Investigate high-value order cancellations
- Increase basket size through product bundles and upselling
- Improve baseline conversion rate outside campaign periods
- Optimize promotional timing based on day-of-week performance
